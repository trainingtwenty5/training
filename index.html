Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our course

How long does the course take to complete?

Although the total duration of the lessons is about 5.5 hours, they are modular and you can return to them anytime. You can take the course at your own pace – there's no set time limit to complete it.

As for the credits, the cost is approximately 0.06 credits per 24 hours for data storage. The rest of the course components are free of charge. The only exception is one analysis-related lesson, but it's not required – you can simply watch it. So the total depends on how many days it takes you to complete the course.

How much does the course cost?

The course costs $49.99, and this gives you lifetime access. This is far less expensive than one-on-one training.

Additionally:

  • You receive unlimited access.
  • If for any reason the platform ceases to exist, you will get access to the materials on another platform or receive the full set of videos and data individually.
  • 30-day money-back guarantee – try it risk-free, love it or get a full refund.
What's the difference between Normal Mode and Summer Mode?

Normal Mode is the full version of the course, including all lessons.

Summer Mode is a shortened version of the course, featuring selected key lessons. It's perfect if you're short on time but want to quickly get familiar with the basics and functionality of the application.

Regarding the Summer Mode and Normal Mode – the way it works is that the knowledge presented in Summer Mode is actually the most essential part of the course. If you skip Summer Mode, you won't find that information repeated in the Normal Mode videos.

How many lessons are in the course?

There are more than 81 lessons in the course. The remaining elements consist of quizzes and assignments.

Do you provide certification?

I can provide certification for the lessons you completed to employers or clients. I have no affiliation with ESRI so any certification provided is not officially endorsed by ESRI.

Are group discounts available?

Yes, I offer group discounts for purchasing multiple seats at the same organization.

Email me at DanielBuchar@training25.pl with the number of seats you plan to purchase to receive a quote with discount codes.

Are consultations available?

Yes, consultations are available for $299.99.

They help resolve issues with application configuration.

Please note:

  • You have one year from the date of purchase to use them.
